About

PlateCheck UK uses OCR via Tesseract.js to read number plates from your camera, then queries the official DVLA Vehicle Enquiry Service for real-time vehicle data.

Data returned includes: Make, Tax Status, MOT Status, Engine CC, Fuel Type, Colour, CO₂ Emissions, Year of Manufacture, and Euro Status.

Note: BHP is not provided by the DVLA API — engine capacity (cc) is available instead. For BHP you'd need a paid third-party API.
